What is COTC?

COTC is the wondrous brain child of a young guy named Don. Standing for Cream Of The Crop, the idea first spawned in young Don's mind around the beginning of his frshman year of college. The idea came about becasue Don was a very good Halo player, so good in fact he began calling himself the Cream Of The Crop meaning the best of the best. Around this same time Don purchased his first camera and started making videos with his friends as well as make Halo 5 montages thus cream of the crop entertainment was born. At first it was just videos but then I started getting into web development so I decided to drop the entertainment because COTC had become so much more!
